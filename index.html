Medical Wellness & Tourism Expo 2026

Date: 20th – 22nd May, 2026
Venue: Mestil Hotel, Kampala, Uganda

Uganda's first ever Medical Wellness & Tourism Expo will bring together the world's top doctors, leading pharmaceutical companies, international medical tourism agencies, and renowned medical equipment manufacturers. The event will feature educational sessions on the latest medical equipment and technology, live demonstrations, and opportunities for networking and partnerships.

Whether you are a patient, healthcare professional, hospital administrator, or industry stakeholder, this expo is designed to empower you with knowledge, connections, and access to the best in global healthcare.

  • Meet and network with world-class doctors and healthcare experts
  • Engage with top pharmaceutical and medical equipment companies
  • Discover the latest medical technologies and innovations
  • Connect with global medical tourism agencies and facilitators
  • Educational sessions on new medical equipment and healthcare tech
  • Workshops, B2B networking, and live demonstrations
  • Opportunities for partnerships, sponsorships, and collaborations
  • Panel discussions on cross-border healthcare, insurance, and patient safety
  • Technical showcases: AI in healthcare, telemedicine, robotic surgery, and digital health records
  • Special focus: Medical logistics, patient transfer, and international accreditation
