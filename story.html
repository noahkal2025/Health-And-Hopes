Our Story

Uganda's first medical tourism agency dedicated to connecting patients to world-class care

How We Started

Health & Hopes Medicare International was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to ensure that every Ugandan patient has access to the best possible medical care, regardless of where that care is located.

We recognized that many Ugandan patients face the heartbreaking reality of limited medical resources for complex conditions. Advanced treatments, specialized surgeries, and cutting-edge medical technology are often unavailable locally, leaving patients and their families feeling helpless.

As Uganda's first medical tourism agency, we pioneered a new path for patients seeking specialized care abroad. We built relationships with world-class hospitals in India, USA, Germany, Canada, UK, Italy, Israel, South Africa, Egypt, and Kenya, creating a bridge between Ugandan patients and the advanced medical care they need.

Message from Our CEO
