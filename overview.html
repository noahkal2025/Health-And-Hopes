Overview of Health & Hopes Medicare International

We support Ugandan patients seeking world-class medical care abroad through expert visa support, hospital coordination, and trusted treatment planning.

What We Do

  • Provide medical tourism support for India, Germany, USA, and partner countries.
  • Handle visa applications, travel planning, hospital admissions, and patient follow-up.
  • Connect patients to accredited healthcare providers for surgeries, diagnostics, and recovery care.
