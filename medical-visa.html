Medical Visa Assistance: Global Destinations

Your trusted partner for effortless medical travel to India, USA, Germany, Canada, UK, Italy, Israel, South Africa, Egypt, and Kenya. We handle the paperwork so you can focus on healing.

Contact Us WhatsApp Us

Many Ugandan patients travel abroad for high-quality medical treatment. While we focus on India for its affordability and expertise, we also connect patients to specialists in USA, Germany, Canada, UK, Italy, Israel, South Africa, Egypt, and Kenya. To access medical care in these countries, specific medical visas are required.


We provide comprehensive financial assessment services to help determine the most suitable hospital and destination based on your financial capability, ensuring you get the best possible care within your budget.

Who Can Apply

  • Ugandan citizens seeking medical treatment in India
  • Patients traveling with up to two medical attendants
  • Applicants holding a valid Ugandan passport

Types of Medical Visas

Medical Visa (MED)

Issued to the patient seeking medical treatment.

Medical Attendant Visa (MED-X)

Issued to accompanying caregivers (blood relatives or guardians).

E-Medical Visa

Available for short-term medical treatments, processed online.

Required Documentation (Overview)

Medical visa applications generally require the following. Our team provides full guidance on all documentation requirements.


  • Valid passport (with at least 6 months validity)
  • Medical referral or treatment recommendation from a local doctor
  • Supporting medical reports (scans, history)
  • Hospital appointment or invitation letter from India
  • Proof of financial capability
  • Passport-size photographs (white background)

Visa Processing & Validity

  • Visas are issued based on the treatment duration recommended by the hospital.
  • Extensions can be arranged if treatment requires more time than initially planned.
  • Processing timelines are typically short once documents are complete and submitted.

Our Medical Visa & Travel Support Services

We assist Ugandan patients with every step of the journey:


  • Medical opinions from leading Indian specialists
  • Selection of accredited hospitals and treating doctors in India
  • Procuring Medical visa invitation letters
  • Treatment cost estimation and financial planning
  • Medical visa application support (form filling, submission)
  • Travel planning and treatment scheduling
  • Accommodation and transport arrangements in India
  • Follow-up coordination after return to Uganda
